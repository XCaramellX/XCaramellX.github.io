Famous Players

Magnus Carlsen

Born November 30th, 1990 in Tonsberg, Norway. GM(Grand Master) Magnus Carlsen is the current world chess champion and to some the best ever to play the game. When he was 13 year old he got a draw game on GM Garry Kasparov and beat GM Anatoly karpov both in the same event.

Both who are regareded as some of the best players in history, a month before he bacame the second-youngest GM in history. He gained the world number-one ranking in 2011 and has been number one since then. He then won the world title in 2013 and has defended it since 2014 and he has won two world titles in rapid games and four times in blitz games.

Alireza Firouzja

Born June 18th, 2003 in Babol, Iran. GM(Grand Master) Alireza Firouzja currently lives in France, he is a world class player and a two-time Iranian champion and European Fischer Random champion.

In 2016 he became the youngest Iranian chess champion as the age of 12 and earned the International Master title. He is the only teenager ranked top 15 as of March 1, 2021 and in late 2019 to early 2020 he won second place in World Rapid Championship.
