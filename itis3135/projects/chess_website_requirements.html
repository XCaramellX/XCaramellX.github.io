Project 1 Webpage Requirements

Client:

Category 1: client is peer classmate.

Project:

A community group to encourage communication about activities and projects.

The website will be on chess and how to play it. By the time they look through the whole site they should know what chess is, how it works, and how to play a few openings.

Users: People who want to understand the basics of chess.

Categories of content:
Information about different chess clubs and their events.
Famous chess players.
Explanation of different chess pieces.
Different chess openings.

Interactivity:
On the homepage there are going to be clickable images that have the different chess pieces on them. They will lead to other pages(or pop-up images) that explain what the piece is and how it is played.
